Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom patches

What's the minimum order quantity?

We have no minimum order requirements! You can order as few as one patch. Whether you need a single custom patch or thousands, we can accommodate your needs.

How long does production take?

Standard production time is 7-10 business days. We also offer rush production options that can reduce turnaround to as little as 3 business days for most orders.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept all common file formats including AI, EPS, PDF, PSD, PNG, and JPG. For best results, vector files (AI, EPS) are preferred. Our design team can help with file conversion if needed.

Can you help with design?

Absolutely! Our design team offers free design assistance. Send us your ideas, sketches, or concepts and we'll create professional patch artwork at no extra cost.

What's the maximum size for patches?

We can create patches up to 12 inches in any dimension. For larger sizes, we recommend our PVC or sublimation options which maintain quality at larger dimensions.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. We offer several shipping options including standard international mail, DHL, and FedEx. Shipping costs and times vary by destination.
